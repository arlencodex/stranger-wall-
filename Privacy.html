Privacy Policy

Anonymity is not a feature — it's the foundation. We built Stranger Wall so you can speak without a name. This policy explains how little we actually need to know.

Last revised · 12 March 2026 — effective for all users. Your continued use of the platform means you accept this policy.

What We Collect

Messages you post on the wall — they are public by design. We do not store your IP address alongside your message. No name, no email, no persistent identifier.

Optional contact — if you email us directly (for example, to request removal of a message), we will only use your address to reply and then delete it. We never add it to any list.

Session data — a temporary cookie to remember your session preferences. It expires when you close your browser.

  • No tracking: we don't use analytics services, pixels, or third-party trackers.
  • No fingerprints: we don't try to identify you across sessions.

Storage & Retention

Messages are stored on our servers as plain text with a timestamp (UTC). They remain visible until the wall naturally scrolls them out — approximately 200 most recent messages are kept. Older messages are permanently deleted — no backup, no archive.

If you want a specific message removed earlier, contact us with the exact text and approximate time. We'll remove it within 48 hours. Because we don't log IPs, we cannot verify ownership except by matching the content.

Sharing & Legal Requests

We never sell, rent, or trade any data. Since we don't collect personal information, there's nothing to sell.

If legally compelled to provide data (court order), we can only hand over the public messages you already posted — no IPs, no emails, no names. We will notify affected users via a public notice on the wall if permitted.

Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data. Because we store no personal data, these rights apply to the messages you posted:

  • Access: you can view your messages on the wall as long as they exist.
  • Erasure: contact us with the exact message — we'll remove it.

For any privacy request, email privacy@strangerwall.xyz — we reply without storing your address.

Children's Privacy

Stranger Wall is not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware that a child has posted on the wall, we will remove the message promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be posted on this page with the revised date. Continued use of Stranger Wall after changes means you accept the updated policy.
